Mission Statement

Monroe College provides professional, career-oriented higher education to students from diverse backgrounds. We proudly offer access and opportunity to motivated students who desire to enrich their lives in a personalized and supportive environment. We provide caring and effective teaching and engage faculty and staff who are passionate, knowledgeable, and dedicated to student achievement. We build on these strengths to prepare graduates for successful careers, advanced education, and lifelong learning.

Our Veterans

At Monroe, we provide the personal attention and an environment that is both challenging and nurturing. A place where your professors know you by name, take an active interest in your academic program and your goals. What's more, Monroe is committed to small classes, tutorial sessions and a fully staffed Learning Resource Center to help you along the way.

The men and women who have enrolled at Monroe after their military service have collaborated with our faculty and administration to produce a veteran-friendly environment. As a result, Monroe College now has one of the largest veteran populations in the New York area.
